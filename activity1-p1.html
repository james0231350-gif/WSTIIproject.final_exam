Ginataang Dahon ng Gabi

A Filipino vegetable dish cooked in coconut milk

Preparation Time: 15 minutes
Cooking Time: 30 minutes
Servings: 4-6 people

Ingredients:

  • Taro leaves (dahon ng gabi), washed and chopped
  • 1/2 kg pork belly, sliced into strips
  • 1/2 kg shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 2 cups coconut milk (first extract)
  • 1 cup coconut cream (second extract)
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons ginger, julienned
  • 3-5 pieces red or green chili peppers
  • 2 tablespoons shrimp paste (bagoong)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Cooking oil

Instructions:

  1. In a large pot, heat oil and saut√© garlic, onion, and ginger until fragrant.
  2. Add the pork belly and cook until lightly browned.
  3. Pour in the coconut milk and bring to a boil. Lower heat and simmer for 15 minutes.
  4. Add the taro leaves and chili peppers. Cook for another 10 minutes.
  5. Add the shrimp and shrimp paste. Stir well.
  6. Pour in the coconut cream and simmer for 5 more minutes.
  7. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  8. Serve hot with steamed rice. Enjoy!
