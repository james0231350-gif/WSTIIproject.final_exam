Chicken Adobo

The Philippines' most iconic savory chicken dish

Preparation Time: 15-20 minutes
Cooking Time: 40 minutes
Servings: 4-7 people

Ingredients:

  • 1 kg chicken pieces (thighs and drumsticks)
  • 1/2 cup soy sauce
  • 1/3 cup white vinegar
  • 1 head garlic, crushed
  • 3 bay leaves
  • 1 teaspoon whole black peppercorns
  • 1 cup water
  • 2 tablespoons cooking oil
  • 1 tablespoon sugar (optional)
  • Salt to taste
  • Green onions for garnish

Instructions:

  1. In a large pot, combine chicken, soy sauce, vinegar, garlic, bay leaves, and peppercorns.
  2. Marinate for at least 30 minutes (or overnight in the fridge for best results).
  3. Add water to the pot and bring to a boil over medium-high heat.
  4. Lower the heat and simmer covered for 30 minutes, turning chicken occasionally.
  5. Remove chicken and set aside. Continue simmering the sauce until it reduces and thickens.
  6. In a separate pan, heat oil and fry the chicken pieces until browned and slightly crispy.
  7. Pour the reduced sauce over the fried chicken.
  8. Add sugar if desired and adjust seasoning with salt.
  9. Garnish with green onions and serve hot with steamed rice. Enjoy!
