Spaghetti

Classic Filipino-style spaghetti with rich tomato sauce

Preparation Time: 15 minutes
Cooking Time: 30 minutes
Servings: 4-6 people

Ingredients:

  • 400g spaghetti pasta
  • 500g ground beef or pork
  • 1 can (400g) tomato sauce
  • 1 can (200g) tomato paste
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 bell pepper, chopped
  • 1 carrot, grated
  • 2 hotdogs, sliced (optional)
  • 2 tablespoons cooking oil
  • 2 cups water or beef broth
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Grated cheese for topping

Instructions:

  1. Cook spaghetti according to package directions. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a large pan, heat oil and saut√© garlic and onion until fragrant.
  3. Add ground meat and cook until browned.
  4. Add bell pepper, carrot, and hotdogs. Cook for 3-4 minutes.
  5. Pour in tomato sauce and tomato paste. Stir well.
  6. Add water or broth, sugar, salt, and pepper. Mix thoroughly.
  7. Simmer for 20-25 minutes, stirring occasionally until sauce thickens.
  8. Serve sauce over cooked spaghetti and top with grated cheese. Enjoy!
