Fried Rice

Delicious fried rice with eggs and vegetables

Preparation Time: 10 minutes
Cooking Time: 10-15 minutes
Servings: 3-4 people

Ingredients:

  • 4 cups cooked rice (preferably day-old rice)
  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • 1/2 cup mixed vegetables (carrots, peas, corn)
  • 1/4 cup green onions, chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/2 cup cooked protein (chicken, shrimp, or pork), diced
  • 3 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon oyster sauce
  • 2 tablespoons cooking oil
  • 1/2 teaspoon sesame oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Heat 1 tablespoon oil in a large wok or pan over high heat.
  2. Pour beaten eggs and scramble quickly. Remove and set aside.
  3. Add remaining oil and saut√© garlic until fragrant.
  4. Add your choice of protein and cook until heated through.
  5. Add mixed vegetables and stir-fry for 2-3 minutes.
  6. Add the cooked rice and break up any clumps. Stir-fry for 3-4 minutes.
  7. Pour in soy sauce, oyster sauce, and sesame oil. Mix well.
  8. Add scrambled eggs back in and toss everything together.
  9. Add green onions, season with salt and pepper.
  10. Serve hot and enjoy!
